DELEGATION or DISRESPECT

One of my direct reports once told me in a 1:1 early in my career as a founder: "I've never felt so disrespected in my career." This was a wake-up call. I wasn't delegating, and we weren't aligned on goals and values. I was micromanaging, and second guessing everyone. In this video we talk about what it takes to be a great leader: a conductor of people who drive greatness.
